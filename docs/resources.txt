Recommended Academic Textbooks:

1. Harris, D., & Harris, S. (2012). *Digital Design and Computer Architecture*. Morgan Kaufmann.
   - A modern approach that covers digital logic design and building a MIPS microprocessor in both Verilog and VHDL.

2. Brown, S., & Vranesic, Z. (2009). *Fundamentals of Digital Logic with VHDL Design*. McGraw-Hill Education.
   - A classic textbook that provides a strong foundation in digital logic principles with a focus on VHDL.

3. Patterson, D. A., & Hennessy, J. L. (2017). *Computer Organization and Design: The Hardware/Software Interface*. Morgan Kaufmann.
   - A widely-used textbook that covers the principles of computer organization and architecture.

4. Mano, M. M., & Ciletti, M. D. (2018). *Digital Design: With an Introduction to the Verilog HDL, VHDL, and SystemVerilog*. Pearson.
   - A comprehensive book that covers the fundamentals of digital design and introduces multiple hardware description languages.

5. Short, K. L. (2008). *VHDL for Engineers*. Pearson/Prentice Hall.
   - A practical guide to learning and using VHDL for digital system design.
